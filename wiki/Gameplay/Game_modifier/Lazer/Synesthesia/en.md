# Synesthesia (lazer mod)

*For the full list of all lazer mods, see: [Game modifier (lazer)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer)*

## About

- Abbreviation: SY
- Type: Fun
- Score Multiplier: 0.80x
- Caption: `Colours hit objects based on the rhythm.`
- Compatible game modes: ![][osu!]
- Incompatible mods: None
- Status: **UNRANKED**

## Gameplay

![](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Mods/Gameplay/synesthesia.gif)

Comparison between a regular play (right) vs a play with the Synesthesia mod enabled (left) in osu!

*Beatmap: [II-L - SPUTNIK-3 [Hard]](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/1312076#osu/2991175)*

## Additional Information

The colours from the **Synesthesia mod** are based on the beat snap from the editor. If an object starts on a specific line, it will have its colour.

![Beat snap from the editor](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/img/synesthesia2.png "1/12 triplets beat map from the beatmap SPUTNIK-3.")
