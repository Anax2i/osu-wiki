# Muted (lazer mod)

![MU mod icon](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Mods/MU.png "Muted (MU) mod icon")

*For the full list of all lazer mods, see: [Game modifier (lazer)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer)*

## About

- Abbreviation: MU
- Type: Fun
- Score Multiplier: 1.00x
- Caption: `Can you still feel the rhythm without music?`
- Compatible game modes: ![][osu!] ![][osu!taiko] ![][osu!catch] ![][osu!mania]
- Incompatible mods: None
- Status: **RANKED**

## Additional Information

The **Muted** mod progressively removes the audio between the first and hundredth combo. Beyond that number, only the metronome can be heard. If you miss an object, everything will be back to normal.
