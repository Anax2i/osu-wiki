## About

- Abbreviation: CN
- Type: Automation
- Score Multiplier: 1.00x
- Default shortcut key: `X`
- Caption: `Watch the video without visual distractions.`
- Compatible game modes: ![][osu!] ![][osu!taiko] ![][osu!catch] ![][osu!mania]
- Incompatible mods: [No Fail (NF)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/No_Fail), [Sudden Death (SD)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Sudden_Death), [Perfect (PF)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Perfect), [Accuracy Challenge (AC)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Accuracy_Challenge), [Autoplay (AT)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Autoplay), [Relax (RX)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Relax), [Autopilot (AP)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Autopilot), [Spun Out (SO)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Spun_Out), [Alternate (AL)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Alternate), [Single Tap (SG)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Single_Tap), [Magnetised (MG)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Magnetised), [Repel (RP)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Repel), [Adaptive Speed (AS)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Adaptive_Speed), [Touch Device (TD)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Touch_Device)
