# Adaptive Speed (lazer mod)

*For the full list of all lazer mods, see: [Game modifier (lazer)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer)*

## About

- Abbreviation: AS
- Type: Fun
- Score Multiplier: 0.50x
- Caption: `Let track speed adapt to you.`
- Compatible game modes: ![][osu!] ![][osu!taiko] ![][osu!mania]
- Incompatible mods: [Half Time (HT)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Half_Time), [Daycore (DC)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Daycore), [Double Time (DT)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Double_Time), [Nightcore (NC)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Nightcore), [Autoplay (AT)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Autoplay), [Cinema (CN)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Cinema), [Wind Up (WU)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Wind_Up), [Wind Down (WD)](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Lazer/Wind_Down)
- Status: **UNRANKED**

## Additional Information

The **Adaptive Speed** mod allows you to change the tempo of the map you're playing depending on how fast you click on the objects. It can vary between 0.50x and 2.00x.
